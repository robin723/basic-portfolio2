About Me

Robin Li or Li Yanhong born 17 November 1968) is a Chinese Internet entrepreneur. He co-founded the Chinese search engine Baidu

Li has been CEO of Baidu since January 2004. The company was listed on NASDAQ on August 5, 2005.[7] Li was included as one of the 15 Asian Scientists To Watch by Asian Scientist Magazine on 15 May 2011
